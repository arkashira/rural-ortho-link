# 🚀 rural-ortho-link

**Empower rural communities with seamless access to orthopedic care through AI-driven service matching.**  
A cloud-native platform that connects underserved rural patients to nearby orthopedic specialists, reducing travel time and improving healthcare access.

## Why rural-ortho-link?
- **Proactive Rural Access**: Proactively connects underserved rural patients to nearby orthopedic specialists, reducing travel time by up to 50%.
- **Built for Orthopedic Clinics**: Orthopedic clinics and rural healthcare providers can expand service reach without increasing operational overhead.
- **AI-Powered Matching**: Machine learning model matches patients to clinics based on medical needs (e.g., fracture type, mobility issues) and location.
- **Real-Time Availability**: Clinics update real-time capacity and wait times, improving decision-making for both patients and providers.
