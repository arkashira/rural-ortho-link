# Business Model Canvas

## Customer Segments
- Rural patients with orthopedic conditions lacking access to specialized care
- Healthcare providers in underserved rural areas needing specialist consultation support
- Orthopedic specialists willing to provide telemedicine services
- Insurance companies covering rural healthcare markets
- Public health organizations addressing healthcare disparities

## Value Propositions
- Access to specialized orthopedic care without geographic barriers
- Off-label therapy prescription options for complex orthopedic cases
- Reduced travel time and costs for rural patients (average savings: $250 USD/8,750 THB per visit)
- Improved health outcomes through timely specialist intervention
- Platform for knowledge sharing between rural providers and specialists

## Channels
- Telemedicine platform (web and mobile applications)
- Partnerships with rural healthcare clinics and community centers
- Direct outreach through agricultural and rural-focused media
- Integration with existing rural healthcare systems
- Referral networks from primary care providers in underserved areas

## Customer Relationships
- Dedicated case management for complex orthopedic cases
- Continuous follow-up care coordination between visits
- Educational resources for patients on treatment options and rehabilitation
- Technical support for healthcare providers using the platform
- Satisfaction surveys and feedback mechanisms for continuous improvement

## Revenue Streams
- Consultation fees (specialist rates: $150-250 USD/5,250-8,750 THB per session)
- Subscription fees for healthcare organizations ($1,000-1,500 USD/35,000-52,500 THB per month)
- Premium services for complex case management and treatment planning
- Data analytics services for healthcare planning and resource allocation
- Insurance billing partnerships for reimbursement services

## Key Resources
- Telemedicine technology platform with HIPAA-compliant security
- Network of credentialed orthopedic specialists
- Medical regulatory compliance framework for cross-border healthcare
- Data security infrastructure for patient information and treatment records
- Rural healthcare partnerships and provider network

## Key Activities
- Platform development and maintenance for telemedicine services
- Specialist recruitment and credentialing verification
- Patient-provider matching algorithm based on medical needs and availability
- Quality assurance for telemedicine consultations and treatment plans
- Regulatory compliance monitoring and adaptation to changing healthcare laws

## Key Partners
- Rural healthcare clinics and hospitals as access points
- Orthopedic specialist associations for credentialing and standards
- Insurance companies for reimbursement and coverage expansion
- Healthcare technology providers for platform enhancement
- Rural community organizations for patient outreach and education

## Cost Structure
- Platform development and maintenance ($75,000 USD/2,625,000 THB annually)
- Specialist compensation ($40-60 USD/1,400-2,100 THB per consultation)
- Marketing and outreach ($35,000 USD/1,225,000 THB annually)
- Regulatory compliance and legal ($20,000 USD/700,000 THB annually)
- Customer support operations ($25,000 USD/875,000 THB annually)
