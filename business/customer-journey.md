## Customer Journey – Rural‑Ortho‑Link  

| Phase | Trigger Event | Primary Friction Points | User Emotions | Opportunities to Delight | Success Metric |
|-------|---------------|--------------------------|---------------|--------------------------|----------------|
| **Aware** | • A farmer injures a knee while working the fields and hears a local radio ad about “Specialist Orthopedic Care – No Travel Required”.<br>• Community health worker posts a flyer in the village clinic. | • Low broadband / spotty cellular coverage.<br>• Skepticism about “online doctors”.<br>• Lack of awareness of insurance coverage for tele‑orthopedics. | Curious → Hopeful but cautious | • Offer a **free 5‑minute video intro** that shows a real orthopedic surgeon speaking in the local dialect.<br>• Provide a **simple bandwidth‑adaptive UI** (text‑first, video fallback).<br>• Highlight **insurance partners** and “no‑out‑of‑pocket” guarantee on the landing page. | **Impression‑to‑Click Rate** – % of ad/flyer viewers who click “Learn More” (target ≥ 12 %). |
| **Consider** | • Patient (or family) visits the Rural‑Ortho‑Link website after hearing the ad and reads case studies of nearby farmers who avoided surgery. | • Unclear pricing & payment flow.<br>• Fear of mis‑diagnosis without physical exam.<br>• Language barrier for medical terminology. | Anxious → Evaluative | • Show a **transparent pricing calculator** with “pay‑as‑you‑go” and “insurance covered” options.<br>• Provide a **virtual “Ask a Nurse” chat** (AI‑augmented, bilingual) to answer quick questions.<br>• Publish **short testimonial videos** filmed on‑site in the same region. | **Consideration Conversion** – % of visitors who start the “Schedule Consultation” flow (target ≥ 8 %). |
| **Try** | • Patient books a 15‑minute tele‑consultation with an orthopedic specialist via the app. | • Difficulty uploading X‑ray / ultrasound images due to limited bandwidth.<br>• Uncertainty about the quality of the connection during the call.<br>• Need for immediate prescription for off‑label therapy (e.g., low‑dose ketamine). | Nervous → Relieved (if call succeeds) | • Offer an **offline image capture** mode that stores locally and uploads when signal improves.<br>• Provide a **real‑time connection health bar** and automatic fallback to audio‑only.<br>• Enable **e‑prescription via secure SMS** that works on basic phones. | **Trial Success Rate** – % of booked consultations that finish without technical abort (target ≥ 85 %). |
| **Adopt** | • After the consult, the patient receives a personalized treatment plan, including physiotherapy videos and an off‑label medication prescription. | • Limited access to physiotherapy equipment.<br>• Concerns about medication side‑effects and legal compliance.<br>• Need for follow‑up monitoring. | Empowered → Cautiously optimistic | • Ship a **low‑cost “home‑rehab kit”** (elastic bands, instructional QR codes).<br>• Provide **regulatory‑clearance documentation** for off‑label use, co‑signed by a local physician.<br>• Schedule **automated weekly check‑ins** (SMS or voice) with a care coordinator. | **Adoption Retention** – % of trial users who complete the first 4‑week treatment plan (target ≥ 70 %). |
| **Expand** | • Patient experiences pain reduction and shares success with neighbors; clinic staff request broader services (e.g., post‑operative follow‑up, joint replacement triage). | • Scaling to multiple family members increases scheduling complexity.<br>• Need for integration with local pharmacy inventory.<br>• Desire for broader specialty coverage (e.g., sports medicine). | Proud → Loyal | • Introduce a **family‑plan subscription** with priority scheduling.<br>• Connect to **local pharmacy APIs** for same‑day medication delivery.<br>• Roll out **cross‑specialty tele‑modules** (sports, rheumatology) under the same brand. | **Expansion Revenue per User** – Avg. additional ARR generated after month 3 (target ≥ $120 / user). |

### Visual Summary (Linear Flow)

```
[Aware] → (ad/flyer) → [Consider] → (pricing, testimonials) → [Try] → (video consult) → 
[Adopt] → (treatment plan, e‑prescribe) → [Expand] → (family plan, extra specialties)
```

---  

**Key Takeaway:**  
Delight hinges on **low‑bandwidth‑first design**, **transparent, bilingual communication**, and **tangible offline support** (rehab kits, SMS prescriptions). By removing technical and trust barriers at each stage, Rural‑Ortho‑Link can move users from curiosity to a multi‑year, multi‑service relationship, unlocking a sustainable revenue stream in underserved rural markets.
