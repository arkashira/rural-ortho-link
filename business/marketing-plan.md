# marketing‑plan.md

## 1. Positioning  
**One‑liner**  
> *“Rural‑Ortho‑Link: The only tele‑orthopedic network that lets rural patients access specialist care and receive off‑label therapy prescriptions in one secure, low‑latency platform.”*

**Three alternative taglines**  
1. *“From farm to recovery – specialist orthopedics at your doorstep.”*  
2. *“Bridging distance, accelerating healing – tele‑orthopedics for rural communities.”*  
3. *“Orthopedic care that travels to you – secure, compliant, and cost‑effective.”*

## 2. Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

| Persona | Daily‑Job | Pain‑Points | Budget (USD) | Decision‑Maker |
|---------|-----------|-------------|--------------|----------------|
| **Dr. Maya Patel** – Rural Primary Care Physician | Manages 120 patients/month, triages referrals | Limited specialist access, high patient travel cost | $3k–$5k/month for tele‑health tools | Medical Director |
| **John “Jake” Thompson** – Rural Hospital Administrator | Oversees 50‑bed community hospital, budget constraints | Low reimbursement for specialist visits, high readmission risk | $10k–$15k/month for tech upgrades | CFO |
| **Lisa Nguyen** – Rural Patient (35‑55, farmer) | Daily farm work, limited time for travel | Cannot afford specialist visits, needs quick prescription | $200–$500/month for health services | Self‑payer |

## 3. Channels & CAC Estimates

| Channel | Strategy | Estimated CAC (USD) | Rationale |
|---------|----------|---------------------|-----------|
| **1. Targeted LinkedIn Ads** | Sponsored InMail to Dr. Patel & Jake’s networks | $120 | High intent, professional audience |
| **2. Rural Health Conferences (e.g., Rural Health Conference 2026)** | Booth + live demo + QR‑code lead capture | $250 | Direct access to decision‑makers |
| **3. Referral Partnerships with Rural Health Associations** | Co‑branded webinars + case studies | $80 | Low cost, high trust |

*Total CAC (average) ≈ $150.*
