# Business Model Canvas – **rural-ortho-link**

| **Block** | **Details** |
|---|---|
| **Value Proposition** | • **Immediate specialist access** – Rural patients connect via video/async chat to board‑certified orthopedic surgeons without traveling long distances.<br>• **AI‑augmented triage** – vLLM‑powered symptom analysis and SGLang‑structured care plans surface likely diagnoses and prioritize urgent cases.<br>• **Off‑label therapy facilitation** – Secure e‑prescribing workflow, backed by evidence‑based protocols, enables timely access to approved off‑label treatments (e.g., biologics, regenerative injections).<br>• **Integrated follow‑up** – Automated outcome surveys, physiotherapy video modules, and remote monitoring reduce readmission.<br>• **Cost reduction** – Lower transportation, missed‑work, and unnecessary imaging for patients; lower referral overhead for rural clinics. |
| **Customer Segments** | 1. **Rural patients & families** – Individuals with musculoskeletal pain, injuries, or chronic joint disease lacking local specialist care.<br>2. **Orthopedic specialists** – Surgeons seeking a tele‑practice channel and supplemental revenue.<br>3. **Rural health clinics & community health workers** – Referral partners that need a reliable specialist network.<br>4. **Payers / Insurance carriers** – Public & private insurers aiming to contain costs while improving outcomes.<br>5. **Pharma & medical‑device firms** – Companies interested in off‑label therapy data, real‑world evidence, and targeted outreach. |
| **Channels** | • **Web portal** (responsive UI) for patients and clinicians.<br>• **Native iOS/Android apps** for video visits, secure messaging, and prescription receipt.<br>• **Clinic integration** – HL7/FHIR APIs that embed rural‑ortho‑link into existing EMR workflows.<br>• **Community outreach** – Partnerships with local health workers, radio ads, and tele‑health fairs.<br>• **Referral marketplace** – Dashboard for clinics to request specialist consults and track status. |
| **Revenue Streams** | 1. **Subscription fee** – Monthly per‑clinic license (tiered by number of active patients).<br>2. **Per‑visit fee** – Fixed reimbursement for each completed specialist tele‑consult (shared 70/30 with surgeon).<br>3. **Prescription processing surcharge** – Small fee on off‑label e‑prescriptions (covers pharmacy logistics & compliance).<br>4. **Data‑analytics services** – Aggregated, de‑identified outcome dashboards sold to insurers & pharma (HIPAA‑compliant).<br>5. **Continuing‑education credits** – Paid webinars for rural clinicians on orthopedic best practices. |
| **Key Resources** | • **AI stack** – vLLM inference engine + SGLang for structured generation of triage notes and care pathways.<br>• **Clinical knowledge base** – Curated orthopedic guidelines, off‑label therapy protocols, and imaging decision trees (licensed from professional societies).<br>• **Regulatory compliance framework** – HIPAA‑ready data pipelines, e‑prescribing (NCPDP) integration, and FDA/EMA off‑label guidance.<br>• **Network of orthopedic surgeons** – Contracted specialists with tele‑medicine credentials.<br>• **Platform infrastructure** – Cloud‑native microservices, pgvector knowledge store, secure video stack (WebRTC). |
| **Key Activities** | 1. **Platform development & maintenance** – Continuous delivery of video, messaging, AI triage, and e‑prescribing modules.<br>2. **Specialist recruitment & credentialing** – Vetting, onboarding, and schedule management for surgeons.<br>3. **Clinical workflow design** – Building evidence‑based pathways for off‑label therapies and post‑visit follow‑up.<br>4. **Compliance & security audits** – Quarterly HIPAA, SOC‑2, and FDA off‑label reviews.<br>5. **Customer acquisition & support** – Outreach to rural clinics, training for health workers, and 24/7 patient help desk. |
| **Key Partners** | • **Orthopedic societies** (AAOS, European Society of Orthopaedic Surgery) – Clinical guideline licensing.<br>• **Rural health networks** – Referral pipelines and community health worker programs.<br>• **Tele‑health platform providers** (e.g., Doxy.me, VSee) – Video SDK integration.<br>• **Pharmacy fulfillment partners** – Secure e‑prescription transmission and medication delivery to remote areas.<br>• **Insurance carriers** – Reimbursement contracts and value‑based care pilots.<br>• **Regulatory consultants** – Ongoing guidance for off‑label prescribing compliance. |
