# STORIES.md

## Epic 1: Patient Onboarding and Scheduling

### User Story 1
As a rural patient, I want to register with basic health information and photo ID, so that I can access the platform securely.
- **Acceptance Criteria**:
  - Fill out registration form with name, date of birth, address, and contact details.
  - Upload a valid photo ID (passport, driver's license).
  - Receive a verification email with a link to confirm registration.
  - Successfully log in after verification.

### User Story 2
As a rural patient, I want to schedule a video consultation with a specialist, so that I can get timely medical advice without travel.
- **Acceptance Criteria**:
  - Browse available specialists by specialty (e.g., orthopedic surgeon) and availability.
  - Select a time slot from the calendar view.
  - Receive a confirmation email with a secure link to join the video call.
  - Notification (email/SMS) 1 hour before the appointment.

### User Story 3
As a rural patient, I want to receive reminders for upcoming appointments, so that I don't miss them.
- **Acceptance Criteria**:
  - Set up email and SMS reminders (configurable frequency: 1 day before, 2 hours before).
  - Option to opt out of reminders.
  - Reminder includes appointment details and link.
